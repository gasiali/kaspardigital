Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is the goal of the game?

A: The goal is simple: control your snake to eat the food pellets that appear on the screen. Each pellet eaten makes your snake grow longer. The game ends when the snake runs into a wall or, more commonly, runs into its own body!
